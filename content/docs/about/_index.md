# About

This website/blog exists as a way for me to organize and share whatever I'm thinking about. Most posts will be related to technical areas, especially machine learning, scientific computing, probabilistic programming, formal methods, and any other mathematical subjects I find intriguing. However, I may occasionally indulge in posts about other technologies or less technical subjects.

Feel free to [reach out]({{< relref "/docs/contact" >}}) if you have similar interests to mine and you want to connect. Opinions on this blog are my own and do not represent those of my employer.
